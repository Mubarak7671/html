Different Types of Cards

Debit Card

A **debit card** is linked directly to your bank account. Money is deducted immediately when you make purchases. It can be used for shopping, ATM withdrawals, and online payments.

Credit Card

A **credit card** lets you borrow money from the bank up to a set limit. You can pay later, either in full or in installments with interest. It is used for shopping, travel bookings, and building a credit history.

Prepade Card

A**prepaid card** is loaded with money in advance and not linked to a bank account. You can spend only the amount available on the card. It is used for online shopping, travel, gifts, and budgeting.

Virtual Card

A**virtual card** is a digital-only card for online transactions. It is linked to your bank account or credit card. It is used for online shopping, subscriptions, and one-time payments.

Business Card

A **business card** is issued to companies or business owners for business expenses. It can be a credit or debit card type. It is used for managing business purchases and employee expenses.

Platinum/Gold/Specialized Cards

**Platinum Card:** Offers premium benefits like higher credit limits, rewards, concierge services, and travel perks. **Gold Card:** Provides better rewards, discounts, and privileges than standard cards, often with moderate annual fees. **Specialized Card:** Designed for specific purposes like travel, shopping, fuel, or lifestyle, offering targeted rewards and benefits.
